The Sparkling Water Hostel is a fictitious hostel located in Caxambu-MG, Brazil. Caxambu is a small town (Its population in 2020 was estimated at 22,000), renowned for its spa wich has twelve sources of sparking mineral water flowing 24 hours a dar and a color-water geyser. Caxambu is the only place in the world with 12 mineral waer springs, each with different chemical properties.
